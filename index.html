🌿 A Note on Vegetarian Options

Vegetarian options will be available at the feast — however, we are only catering for guests who have specifically told us they require a vegetarian meal. If you haven't let us know in advance, please be aware that choosing the vegetarian option would be taking a plate from someone who has been specifically catered for. If you'd like a vegetarian meal, please make sure it is noted on your RSVP.

Journeying to Rylands

📍 The Longhouse

Rylands Farmhouse
Just off Altrincham Road, Wilmslow, Cheshire
Postcode: SK9 4LT

Small road next left past The Honey Bee Pub, just off Altrincham Road (leading to bank house farm) — sharp left!

Around 10 minutes from Manchester Airport. Follow postcode SK9 4LT and look out for The Honey Bee Pub — take the small road to the left just after it, sharp left toward bank house farm.

The nearest station is Wilmslow Station, just a short taxi ride from the venue. Perfect for those travelling from Manchester or the surrounding area.

There are limited parking spaces at the venue. We kindly ask guests to share cars where possible, or arrange taxis if staying nearby.

Please liaise with Mel or Mike directly if you will be driving so they can coordinate spaces.
