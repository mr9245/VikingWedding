Dress for the Saga

ᚾ

Embrace the Viking spirit — here is your guide to the theme and where to find your war garb

⚔ The Look

Think earthy tones — deep browns, forest greens, charcoal greys, burnt reds and natural creams. Layered tunics, woollen cloaks, leather belts and boots, fur trim, braided hair, and silver jewellery all fit the theme perfectly. Whether you go full shieldmaiden or just add a few Norse touches to your outfit, it all adds to the atmosphere.

Men

Tunics • Trousers • Cloaks • Fur vests • Leather bracers • Boots • Horned or nasal helms • Axes (decorative!)

Women

Apron dresses • Linen underdresses • Woollen shawls • Cloaks • Norse brooches • Braided updos • Leather boots

Colours

Forest green • Earthy brown • Deep red • Charcoal • Natural cream • Burnt orange • Navy • Muted gold

Remember — your presence matters far more than your attire. Come as you are and join the revelry!
